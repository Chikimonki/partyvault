### Competitors you'd face:
- **GLEIF** (Global LEI Foundation) — but they're a utility, not innovating fast
- **Refinitiv/LSEG** — entity data, but bloated enterprise
- **Bloomberg** — entity data within terminal ecosystem
- **Moody's/Bureau van Dijk (Orbis)** — corporate data, not crypto-native
- **Startups**: Quantexa, Encompass, Cynopsis — but focused on AML screening, not identity infrastructure

### Your gap to exploit:
**Nobody is building cryptographically verifiable, real-time party identity infrastructure purpose-built for the post-digitization capital markets.** The incumbents are all pre-digital-era data aggregators. You'd be building for the world that started on March 16, 2025.

That's a real opportunity. The mockup above is your first step toward demonstrating it.
